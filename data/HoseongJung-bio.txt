Hoseong Jung is a Ph. D. student in Interdisciplinary Program in Artificial Intelligence at Seoul National University. He received the B. S. degree in electrical and computer engineering from Soul National University in 2021. He was an active Naval Officer and has been with the Agency for Defense Development (ADD) from 2021 to 2024. His current research interests include developing multi-agent reinforcement learning systems and applying them to various decision-making scenarios.
